SECOND STORY When old Mr Fox was dead, the wolf came as a suitor, and knocked at the door, and the cat who was servant to Mrs Fox, opened it for him. The wolf greeted her, and said: ‘Good day, Mrs Cat of Kehrewit, How comes it that alone you sit? What are you making good?’ The cat replied: ‘In milk I’m breaking bread so sweet, Will you be my guest, and eat?’ ‘No, thank you, Mrs Cat,’ answered the wolf. ‘Is Mrs Fox not at home?’ The cat
