The doctor looked at the dish, had no idea what to say, and cried: ‘Ah, poor Crabb.’ When the lord heard that, he cried: ‘There! he knows it; he must also know who has the money!’ On this the servants looked terribly uneasy, and made a sign to the doctor that they wished him to step outside for a moment.
