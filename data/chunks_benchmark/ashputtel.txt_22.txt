But her mother squeezed it in till the blood came, and took her to the king’s son: and he set her as his bride by his side on his horse, and rode away with her. But when they came to the hazel-tree the little dove sat there still, and sang: ‘Back again! back again! look to the shoe! The shoe is too small, and not made for you!
